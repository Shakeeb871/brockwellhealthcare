Where Patients Trust Regenerative Care

Regenerative Medicine for Patients Across the UAE

Brockwell Healthcare is based in Dubai and cares for patients across the United Arab Emirates. In-person consultations take place in Dubai, with patients travelling to us from Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah and Umm Al Quwain.

For eligible follow-ups, prescription reviews and non-emergency assessments, secure telehealth is available across the UAE. Diagnostic procedures and hands-on treatments are completed in person in Dubai.
