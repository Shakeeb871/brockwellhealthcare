Brockwell Healthcare is a regenerative medicine and longevity clinic in Dubai, built on more than 25 years of clinical experience. We help patients overcome chronic pain, injury and age-related decline through advanced, non-surgical medicine that looks for the root cause behind a problem.

{# ---------- Who we are ---------- #}
Our Clinic

A Dubai-Based Regenerative Medicine & Longevity Clinic

Brockwell brings regenerative medicine, longevity care, diagnostics and functional medicine together under one clinical model in Dubai. Our focus is the root cause of pain, dysfunction and age-related decline, addressed through evidence-based, non-surgical protocols.

Patients come to Brockwell with chronic pain, sports and musculoskeletal injuries, slow recovery, age-related decline, and hormonal or metabolic concerns. Not every condition suits regenerative medicine, and emergency or acute surgical cases sit outside our scope, so an honest assessment always comes first.

Every patient is different, so every care plan is too. Each one begins with a proper clinical assessment, clear medical reasoning and a treatment path designed around the person in front of us.

Our specialists are board-certified and internationally trained. Together, they bring globally recognised regenerative and longevity medicine to patients across the UAE who want lasting improvements in how they move, recover and age.

{# ---------- The Brockwell Approach ---------- #}
How We Work

The Brockwell Approach

Care at Brockwell follows a clear, consistent path from the first visit to long-term follow-up.

  1. 1

    Consultation

    Your specialist reviews your history, symptoms, goals and past treatments in detail.

  2. 2

    Diagnostics

    Where needed, imaging and testing confirm the root cause before any treatment begins.

  3. 3

    Personalised Plan

    Your doctor designs a non-surgical plan around your condition, your safety and the outcomes you can realistically expect.

  4. 4

    Treatment & Follow-Up

    You receive precise, evidence-based care, and follow-up reviews track your progress and adjust the plan as you recover.

Meet the Founder

Dr. Hasnain Haider-Shah

Founder & Lead Specialist · American Board Trained · Neurointerventional & Regenerative Medicine
